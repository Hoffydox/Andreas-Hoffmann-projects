Sophie Augusta

An Art Portfolio by Sophie Augusta

Watercolour, ceramics, line drawings

Hello, I'm a young artist living in the heart of Aalborg. I create art with meaningful messages expressed in anything from beautiful landscapes to bleeding vagina cups.

Melt - 2018
Loss - 2017
Reflection - 2016

My art explores a lot of dark abstract themes which are open for interpertation.

My inspiration comes from undefined, dark emotions or sensations in life. I mostly explore these themes in my line drawings, sometimes accompanied by watercolours.

Apart from the line drawings, I also experiment with a natural portait-inspired style, and the fantasy-like universe, which watercolours can create.

Cosmic taste - 2017
Flow - 2019
Pregnant Pussycup - 2020

One of my recent projects is the pussy pottery.
This is a series of cups that depicts different types of vaginas and challenge the narrow representation and inclusion of this bodypart.
Bodypositivism is also a source of inspiration for many of my other works.
